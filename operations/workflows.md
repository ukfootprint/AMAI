## Weekly Review

**Purpose:** Reconnect with your goals, clear your head, and set up the week ahead.

**When:** [Fill in: Day and time — e.g. "Friday afternoon or Monday morning"]

**Steps:**
1. Open `goals/current_focus.yaml` — review active priorities and update status
2. Open `memory/decisions.jsonl` — log any significant decisions made this week
3. Open `goals/goals.yaml` — am I on track? What needs attention?
4. Open `network/interactions.jsonl` — log any significant conversations
5. Check `operations/rituals.md` — did I stick to my commitments this week?
6. Set the "one thing" for next week in `goals/current_focus.yaml`

**Time required:** 30–45 minutes

---

## Monthly Review

**Purpose:** Zoom out from weekly execution to strategic assessment.

**When:** [Fill in: e.g. "Last working day of the month"]

**Steps:**
1. Review all `goals/` files — are goals still the right ones?
2. Update `knowledge/domain_landscape.md` with anything significant that shifted
3. Review `network/contacts.jsonl` — any relationships that need attention?
4. Add significant experiences or decisions to `memory/`
5. Sweep `goals/backlog.md` — anything ready to activate?
6. Run calibration review *(if using advanced layer)* — see `calibration/MODULE.md`

**Time required:** 60–90 minutes

---

## New Opportunity Evaluation

**When:** A potential partnership, product feature, or strategic opportunity appears.

**Steps:**
1. Write the opportunity down in one sentence
2. Check `goals/north_star.md` — does this move toward the vision?
3. Check `goals/current_focus.yaml` — does this distract from what's already in motion?
4. Apply relevant principles from `identity/principles.md`
5. If still unclear, add to `goals/backlog.md` and review in 2 weeks

---

## Writing an Important Communication

**Before writing:**
1. Load `identity/voice.md` — are you in the right voice?
2. Check `network/contacts.jsonl` for context on the recipient
3. Apply the Pyramid Principle: conclusion first, then reasoning

**Structure:**
- Line 1: The point of the message
- Lines 2–3: Why it matters to them
- Final line: The specific ask or next step
